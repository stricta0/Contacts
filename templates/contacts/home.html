CSV format

The file must be a CSV with a header row and columns in this exact order:

first_name,last_name,email,phone,city,status

Example:

first_name,last_name,email,phone,city,status
Jan,Kowalski,jan.kowalski@example.com,+48 600 700 800,Wrocław,new
Anna,Nowak,anna.nowak@example.com,+48 500 400 300,Warszawa,in_progress
  • status must match one of the values returned by /api/contacts/statuses/
  • Use UTF-8 encoding (recommended)
  • One contact per line
